# Internship Opportunities

Narzary Engineering & Consultancy Pvt. Ltd. offers internship programs for students and recent graduates seeking hands-on experience in engineering, technology, planning, and consultancy. Our internships provide real-world exposure, mentorship, and opportunities to contribute to impactful projects.

## Why Intern With Us?

- Work on live projects in civil engineering, IT, planning, architecture, and consultancy
- Learn from experienced professionals and industry leaders
- Gain practical skills and professional development
- Flexible internship durations and project assignments
- Certificate of completion and potential for full-time employment

## Internship Areas

- Civil Engineering & Construction
- Information Technology & Software Development
- Planning & Design
- Project Management
- Business Operations & Support

## Who Can Apply?

- Undergraduate and postgraduate students
- Recent graduates
- Candidates from engineering, IT, architecture, planning, and related fields

## How to Apply

Visit our internships portal or contact our HR team to explore current internship openings and application procedures. Join us to kickstart your career and make a meaningful impact in the industry.
