# Referral Opportunities

Narzary Engineering & Consultancy Pvt. Ltd. welcomes referrals from individuals, professionals, and organizations. If you refer a project to us and it results in a successful engagement, you will earn a percentage of the project value as a referral reward.

## How It Works

1. Refer a client or project to Narzary Engineering & Consultancy Pvt. Ltd. in engineering, IT, planning, consultancy, or related fields.
2. Our team will evaluate the referral and engage with the client.
3. If the referral leads to a signed contract and project execution, you will receive a referral commission based on the agreed percentage.

## Eligible Referrals

- Civil engineering projects
- IT and software development projects
- Planning and design assignments
- Consultancy and project management opportunities
- Government, private sector, and individual clients

## Why Refer to Us?

- Transparent and fair referral process
- Competitive commission rates
- Timely payout upon project confirmation
- Opportunity to build long-term partnerships

## How to Refer

Contact our business development team with your referral details. We will guide you through the process and keep you updated on the status of your referral.

Join our referral program and earn rewards for helping us grow and deliver value to more clients.
