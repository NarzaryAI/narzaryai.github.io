# Civil Engineering Works

Narzary Engineering & Consultancy Pvt. Ltd. provides end-to-end civil engineering services to individuals, government bodies, corporations, and organizations in India and abroad. Our expertise spans construction, infrastructure, roads, bridges, and all facets of project management and consultancy.

## Our Civil Engineering Service Offerings

- **Constructions:** Complete construction services for buildings, infrastructure, and public works.
- **Infrastructures:** Planning, design, and delivery of infrastructure projects including water supply, drainage, roads, and utilities.
- **Buildings / Houses:** Residential, commercial, and institutional building design and construction.
- **Roads & Bridges:** Design, construction, and maintenance of roads, highways, and bridges.
- **Estimation & Costing:** Tender-ready BOQs, budget projections, and quantity surveying for civil projects.
- **DPR Preparation:** Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) and compliance documentation for government and private sector projects.
- **Planning & Design:** Urban, site, and infrastructure planning; architectural and engineering design; master planning and smart city solutions.
- **Construction Supervision & Management:** Quality assurance, contract management, and lifecycle support for ongoing projects.
