# Citizen Services

Narzary Engineering & Consultancy Pvt. Ltd. provides a range of citizen services to help individuals, businesses, and organizations access and benefit from government schemes and digital processes. Our team offers expert assistance and end-to-end support for various applications and compliance needs.

## Our Citizen Service Offerings

- **ITR Filings:** Assistance with Income Tax Return (ITR) filing for individuals, professionals, and businesses.
- **Income Tax Exemption (ST):** Guidance and application support for income tax exemption under Section ST and other relevant provisions.
- **Digital Signature Certificate (DSC):** Application, renewal, and management of Digital Signature Certificates for secure online transactions and e-governance.
- **e-Tendering Participation:** Support for registration, documentation, and participation in government e-tendering processes.
- **Government Schemes:** Application and advisory for various central and state government schemes, subsidies, and benefits.
- **Training & Skill Development:** Access to government-sponsored training, skill development, and certification programs.
- **Other Citizen Services:** Support for PAN, Aadhaar, GST registration, and other essential government services.
