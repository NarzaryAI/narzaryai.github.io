# Planning & Design Services

Narzary Engineering & Consultancy Pvt. Ltd. offers a full suite of planning and design services to individuals, government bodies, corporations, and organizations in India and abroad. Our multidisciplinary team brings creativity, technical expertise, and practical experience to every project.

## Our Planning & Design Service Offerings

- **Architectural Design:** Innovative and functional architectural solutions for residential, commercial, and institutional projects.
- **Town/City Planning:** Urban and regional planning, master planning, land use planning, and smart city solutions.
- **Site Development:** Site analysis, layout planning, infrastructure design, and development strategies.
- **Interior & Exterior Design:** Creative interior and exterior design, decoration, and space optimization for all property types.
- **Space Planning:** Efficient space utilization, workplace design, and adaptive reuse strategies.
- **Feasibility Studies & DPRs:** Preparation of feasibility studies, Detailed Project Reports (DPRs), and compliance documentation.
- **Environmental & Regulatory Planning:** Environmental impact assessments, regulatory compliance, and sustainable design solutions.
