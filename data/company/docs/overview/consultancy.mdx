# Consultancy Services

Narzary Engineering & Consultancy Pvt. Ltd. offers expert consultancy services to individuals, government bodies, corporations, and organizations in India and abroad. Our consultancy practice is built on deep domain expertise, rigorous project management, and a commitment to delivering value and excellence.

## Our Consultancy Offerings

- **Project Estimation & Costing:** Accurate estimation, budgeting, and tender-ready BOQs for civil engineering and infrastructure projects.
- **Quantity Surveying:** Detailed quantity take-offs, cost analysis, and material management for efficient project delivery.
- **Project Management:** End-to-end management of construction, infrastructure, and development projects, including scheduling, resource allocation, and contract administration.
- **Technical Consultancy:** Expert advice on engineering challenges, feasibility studies, and technical solutions for complex projects.
- **DPR Preparation:** Comprehensive Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) for government and private sector projects, including design, planning, and compliance documentation.
- **Planning & Design Consultancy:** Town planning, urban and regional planning, master planning, site development, and smart city solutions.
- **Environmental & Feasibility Studies:** Environmental impact assessments, feasibility studies, and regulatory compliance support.
